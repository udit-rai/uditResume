A short poem- Indian roots and casteism

Published 06 Aug 2018

"I realise this post is after a long, long time (more than an year to be exact), and I offer my sincerest apologies for the same. The reason is I was quite busy with school work (cleared my 12th grade this year). And so I return. This isn't going to be a blog technically, rather a piece of prose I came up with during a long and arduous road trip. I take the liberty to embrace my Indian roots here and take a little jab at casteism (which is why it is in Hindi) :- Ek baar ek jaankar ne mujhse pucha - ""Bhai teri jati Kya hai? Mai bola ""Bhai Kya hi kahu apni jati ke bareme? Aryabhatt bhi humse hai, Chanakya bhi humse hai; Ganit bhi humse hai, vigyan bhi humse hai; Talvar bhi humse h, Vyapar bhi humse h; Bhay(Fear) bhi humse h, sneh bhi humse hi hai; Janab hum hai Indian; Itihas bhi humse tha, Vartman bhi humse hai, aur Bhavishya bhi humse hi hoga"
